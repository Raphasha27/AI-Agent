# ☀️ Case Study: Solar Lead Qualification Agent
**Stack:** Retell AI + n8n + Future AGI + Airtable

## 🔴 The Problem
Solar businesses are flooded with leads, but many are "fake" or "unqualified" (wrong roof type, low electricity bills, etc.). Sales teams waste 60% of their time filtering these leads manually.

## 🤖 The Solution
We built an end-to-end Voice AI Lead Qualification Agent that handles the heavy lifting:

1.  **Call Initiation:** When a lead submits a form, **Retell AI** initiates an immediate, human-like discovery call.
2.  **Smart Discovery:** The AI asks targeted qualification questions (e.g., "Are you the homeowner?", "What's your average monthly bill?").
3.  **Real-Time Processing:** Upon call completion, a webhook triggers an **n8n workflow**.
4.  **Intelligence Layer:** **Future AGI** analyzes the full transcript to determine if the lead meets the "Qualified" criteria.
5.  **CRM Synchronization:** The result (Qualified/Unqualified) and the full summary are instantly pushed to **Airtable**.

## ✅ Results
*   **Zero Wasted Time:** Sales teams only see "Qualified" leads in their pipeline.
*   **Automated Decision Making:** No manual checking or guesswork.
*   **Scalability:** The system can handle 100+ calls simultaneously.
