# 🏆 Top 3 Advanced n8n Automation Templates

Future AGI is proud to offer these verified, production-ready n8n templates designed to solve complex business bottlenecks at scale.

## 1. Facebook & IG Auto-Poster
**Stack:** Google Sheets + OpenAI + Human Approval Gate
*   **How it works:** Pulls content ideas from a Google Sheet, uses OpenAI to generate on-brand captions/hashtags, and pauses for a **Human Approval Gate** via a custom n8n form before posting to Facebook and Instagram.
*   **Value:** Complete control over AI output with zero-manual-scheduling.

## 2. Instagram News Generator
**Stack:** RSS Feeds + GPT-4o + Instagram
*   **How it works:** Monitors industry-specific RSS feeds. When new content drops, GPT-4o synthesizes the news into a carousel or post format and deploys it directly to Instagram.
*   **Value:** Stay 100% relevant and first-to-market with industry news.

## 3. SEO Strategy Swarm
**Stack:** 6-Agent Team + Live SerpAPI Data + GPT-4
*   **How it works:** Orchestrates a "swarm" of 6 specialized agents (Keyword Researcher, Competitor Analyst, Content Strategist, etc.) using live search data from SerpAPI to generate a comprehensive 12-month SEO roadmap.
*   **Value:** Professional-grade SEO consulting automated in 5 minutes.
