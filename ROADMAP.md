This roadmap outlines the journey from foundations to **Agentic Engineering**, designed for the modern Full Stack Developer.

## ┃ ┣ 📂 Frontend Development
*   **Modern JS (ES6+):** The language of interaction.
*   **React.js / Next.js:** High-performance UI frameworks.
*   **State Management:** Redux, Zustand for complex data.
*   **UI Libraries:** Tailwind CSS, Material UI for speed.

## ┃ ┣ 📂 Backend Development
*   **Node.js (Express/Fastify):** Real-time backend logic.
*   **Python (FastAPI/Django):** Data-heavy and AI-centric backend.
*   **API Development:** REST, GraphQL for unified access.
*   **Authentication:** JWT, OAuth (Secure by default).

## ┃ ┣ 📂 Databases
*   **SQL (PostgreSQL):** Relational data and complex queries.
*   **NoSQL (Redis, MongoDB):** Fast caching and flexible storage.
*   **ORMs (Prisma):** Safe and rapid database interaction.

## ┃ ┣ 📂 DevOps & Deployment
*   **Docker:** Professional containerization.
*   **CI/CD (GitHub Actions):** Automation of all checks and deployments.
*   **Cloud (AWS/Vercel):** Production hosting at scale.

## ┃ ┣ 📂 AI Integration

*   **Vector Databases:** FAISS, Pinecone for RAG.
*   **Agentic Engineering:** Orchestrating teams of AI agents.
