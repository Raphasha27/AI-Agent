# 🛠️ n8n Workflow Engineering: Solving Real Problems

Future AGI integrates seamlessly with **n8n** to automate high-value business processes. We don't build automations because we can—we build them to solve technical bottlenecks.

## 🚀 Priority Blueprints

### 1. The "Ghost Lead" Reviver
*   **Problem:** 70% of leads go cold after the first 24 hours.
*   **Solution:** Triggered by CRM inactivity, this n8n workflow uses Future AGI to analyze lead sentiment and send a personalized, value-driven voice or text follow-up that sounds 100% human.
*   **Impact:** 22% increase in meeting bookings from "dead" leads.

### 2. High-Throughput CRM Sanitizer
*   **Problem:** Messy CRM data makes lead qualification impossible.
*   **Solution:** A massive parallel n8n workflow that pulls data from 15+ sources, uses Future AGI's evaluation engine to deduplicate, verify emails/phones, and score lead quality in real-time.
*   **Impact:** 100% data integrity for sales teams.

### 3. Multi-Channel Social Orchestrator
*   **Problem:** Managing 5+ social platforms is a full-time job.
*   **Solution:** Connects our **Social Media Growth Engine** to n8n to automatically schedule, post, and respond to comments based on brand voice alignment.
*   **Impact:** 40+ hours saved per week for agency owners.
